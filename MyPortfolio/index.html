What I do

Design + Front-End Development

I start with planning and iterating a site's structure and layout. Once the proper information architecture is in place, I design the visual layer to create a beautiful user experience. Full Stack Web Applications

I work with both the front and back ends of websites and applications and can tackle projects that involve SQL and NoSQL databases, UI, development frameworks, server-side code, integration to other systems, and quality assurance.
